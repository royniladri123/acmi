Advisory Board

Michael Wiedder

Advisor

Michael Wiedder has almost 3 decades of experience in the Direct Sales Industry. In addition he was a pioneer in the early Internet, producing one of the industry’s 1st tradeshow and conferences starting in 1994. He has also founded one of the 1st online advertising agency’s specializing in the franchise space. His clients included Budget Blinds, Supercuts and Cost cutters.

In the Direct Sales Industry, Michael has funded 3 Direct Sales Company’s. He has also served as CEO of an International Direct Sales company with representatives in over 20 countries and served as a consultant for several other Direct Sale’s industry leading Company’s.

Mr Wiedder is also involved in the non-profit sector. He has served on the community board for the Hollenbeck Youth Center in East Los Angeles. He has sponsored their events and produced the Inner-City games expo for them in his 25 year relationship with the youth center.

In addition, he founded The Fundraising Journal to help the Non-profit Industry and is launching Heroes and Hope in 2020. Heroes and Hope, provides people, who are experiencing a life changing illness, with mentors that have survived a similar illness.

Michael stated, “ I am very excited about Atteva. Providing all natural products to help family’s improve their health and wellness is something I am passionate about. I look forward to impacting our customer’s lives with our great products and also providing an opportunity to help our brand partners be successful in their businesses.”
